Meta viewport zoom

This is to manually test whether the following <meta name="viewport" /> attributes disable zoom: 

<meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1, maximum-scale=1" />

Assumptions: this shouldn't disable zoom on iOS devices since iOS 10, but should still disable zoom on Android.

Preventing users from zooming pages up to at least 200% fails WCAG 2.2 AA success criterion 1.4.4. Resize Text.

From: Vercel Interface Guidelines issue 11
